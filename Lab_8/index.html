Determining the "world's best song" is subjective and varies greatly based on personal preferences, cultural backgrounds, and musical tastes. Music is a highly diverse and evolving art form with countless genres, styles, and artists, each appealing to different audiences.


Some songs are widely recognized and acclaimed globally for their impact, creativity, and popularity. However, what one person considers the best song may not be the same for another.


  • "Bohemian Rhapsody" by Queen
  • "Imagine" by John Lennon
  • "Hey Jude" by The Beatles
  • "Smells Like Teen Spirit" by Nirvana
  • "Hotel California" by Eagles
  • "Stairway to Heaven" by Led Zeppelin
  • "Billie Jean" by Michael Jackson
  • "Like a Rolling Stone" by Bob Dylan
  • "Shape of You" by Ed Sheeran
